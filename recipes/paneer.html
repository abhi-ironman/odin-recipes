Kadai Paneer 🫕




Description

Kadai Paneer is a spicy North Indian curry made with cottage cheese, capsicum, onions, and tomatoes cooked in a traditional kadai (wok).
Infused with bold spices and herbs, it’s a restaurant-style favorite. 🌿

Ingredients 🌶️

Main

  • Paneer – 250g, cubed
  • Bell peppers – 1 cup, diced
  • Onion – 1, diced
  • Tomatoes – 3, pureed
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp

Spices

  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Kashmiri chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1.5 tsp
  • Garam masala – ½ tsp
  • Kasuri methi – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste

Other

  • Oil or ghee – 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander – for garnish

Steps 🔥

1. Sauté Paneer

  1. Lightly pan-fry paneer cubes and keep aside.

2. Make Gravy

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste, then tomato puree and cook well.
  3. Stir in all dry spices and cook until oil separates.

3. Add Veggies

  1. Add bell peppers and sauté for 2–3 minutes.

4. Combine

  1. Add fried paneer and kasuri methi.
  2. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring gently.

5. Serve

  1. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or roti. 🍽️
